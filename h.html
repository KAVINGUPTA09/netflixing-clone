Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Go to your Account Settings and click on "Reset Password". You will receive an email with instructions.

Q: How do I change my subscription plan?

A: Visit the Plans & Pricing page, then choose your preferred plan and confirm the change.

Q: How do I cancel my membership?

A: Navigate to Membership & Billing, then click "Cancel Membership". Follow the prompts to confirm.

Q: Why is my video buffering?

A: This may be due to a slow internet connection. Try restarting your router or reducing video quality in settings.

Q: How can I contact customer support?

A: You can email us directly or visit the "Need Help?" section below.
